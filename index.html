About Struktiv

Built by a Team Obsessed with Construction

Struktiv is created by a team of passionate developers who understand the real pain points in construction project management. We've spent time talking to builders in Bengaluru from mid-sized firms managing 5-10 projects to enterprise teams scaling across the city.

What we discovered: most construction teams are drowning in WhatsApp messages, outdated Excel sheets, and manual compliance tracking. Delays go unnoticed until it's too late. RERA penalties pile up. Projects slip by weeks.

We built Struktiv to change that. Our mission is simple: Empower the next generation of Indian builders to deliver projects faster, stay RERA compliant, and harness the power of data without complexity or overhead.
